Purchase Order Redirection Flow

  1. The Inventory App provides all necessary master data such as items, vendors, and purchase configurations.
  2. The Meslova App uses this master data to create and manage new Purchase Orders (POs).
  3. Once a PO is created and approved in the Meslova App, it is automatically synchronized and returned to the Inventory App for further inventory processing or tracking.
